GlobalData Wisdom is the leading market intelligence service, providing 200 million standardized, comparable market statistics, analysis, reports, survey findings, and news across more than 50 sectors

Why Wisdom

GlobalData Wisdom enables our clients to make better and more timely decisions by delivering the data, expert insights and client support that alternative providers cannot.

  • Direct access to our experts via the ‘Ask an Analyst’ function, enabling clients to rapidly clarify data queries
  • Robust research methodologies and sources, actively encouraging client interaction to ensure total transparency and trust
  • Proprietary analytical frameworks to transform complex data into clear, actionable insights
  • Alternative segmentations providing fresh perspectives on markets and new targets
  • Daily price intelligence, monitoring online pricing activity across more than 340,000 products
  • One single platform, making it easy for users to find the answers they are looking for, in the way they want
  • Proactive support across data interrogation, extraction, and presentation building, offering clients unparalleled support towards building a business case
